- Link: https://www.mdpi.com/1660-4601/19/3/1780
- Purpose: Useful as a broad theory paper for burnout definition, measurement, and research background.

- Purpose: Useful for understanding burnout through exhaustion and disengagement.

->The conceptualization and measurement of burnout
- Link: https://www.wilmarschaufeli.nl/publications/Schaufeli/240.pdf

->The Job Demands-Resources Model of Burnout
- Link: https://www.wilmarschaufeli.nl/publications/Schaufeli/160.pdf

->Job Demands-Resources Theory
- Link: https://www.isonderhouden.nl/doc/pdf/arnoldbakker/articles/articles_arnold_bakker_344.pdf
